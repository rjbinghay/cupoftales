About Cup of Tales

A coffee-break library for short stories.

Cup of Tales is a platform for sharing short stories that can be read in one sitting. Think of it as a coffee-break library.

Our Mission

We believe in supporting writers directly. Unlike other platforms that take commissions, writers on Cup of Tales keep 100% of tips from readers.

Features

  • Stories designed for 5–15 minute reads
  • Direct support to writers (0% commission)
  • Transparent AI labeling
  • Clean, distraction-free reading experience
